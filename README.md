NijaHealth Systems — Healthcare Operations Analytics
 
## Project Overview
An end-to-end healthcare analytics project analysing
500+ patient records across 5 Nigerian hospitals.
Covers patient outcomes, financial performance, staff
operations, and inventory management.
 
Built by: Rasheed A. Tijani

Tools: Excel  |  MySQL  |  Power BI

Dataset: 500 patients, 5 hospitals, 2 years (2023-2024)

## 💡 Key Business Insights
 
- Total revenue across all hospitals: ₦4.6B (2023-2024)
- Readmission rate highest in Emergency dept (18.3%)
- Abuja National Hospital leads profit margin at 33.7%
- Overall bill collection rate: 86.7% (target: 90%)
- Avg patient satisfaction: 3.75 / 5.0
- Stroke and Oncology patients: longest avg stay (16 days)
